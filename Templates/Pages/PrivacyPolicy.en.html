Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Welcome to TheChatbot. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our chatbot services via WhatsApp.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with our service, we collect the following types of information:

  • Identity Data: Your name and phone number as provided by WhatsApp.
  • Authentication Data: Google OAuth tokens (Access and Refresh tokens) to authenticate you and access your Google Services on your behalf.
  • Communication Data: The history of your messages sent to TheChatbot.
  • Usage Data: IDs of Google Spreadsheets you configure for expense tracking.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the conversational service via WhatsApp.
  • Authenticate your identity using Google Accounts.
  • Manage your financial data in your own Google Sheets (reading and writing data) upon your request.
  • Process your messages using Artificial Intelligence to provide relevant responses.

3. Third-Party Service Providers

We share data with specific third-party providers to facilitate our service:

  • Meta (WhatsApp): To send and receive messages.
  • Google: For authentication and to access your Google Sheets. We store tokens securely to maintain this connection.
  • OpenAI: We send message content to OpenAI to generate intelligent responses. Please refer to OpenAI's data policy for more details.

4. Data Retention and Security

Your data, including chat logs and authentication tokens, is stored securely in our database. We retain this information to maintain the context of your conversations and to ensure the continuous operation of services like expense tracking.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data (including chat history and credentials) directly through the chatbot commands or by contacting us.

6. Google User Data

Our use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
